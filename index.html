Our main technologies

React & Angular

With React and Angular, we build attractive, responsive, efficient, and optimized-performance websites, ensuring your brand shines in the digital world.

Golang

With the power and efficiency of Golang, we specialize in crafting agile and robust APIs. Leveraging advanced frameworks like Ginkgo, we ensure reliable and efficient testing, delivering high-performance solutions of unwavering quality.

Unity

Unity is the heart of our game production. Through this powerful platform, we create cross-platform gaming experiences that captivate and immerse players, regardless of their chosen device.

Ruby on Rails

The robustness and efficiency of Ruby, coupled with the Rails framework, form the formula we employ to craft sophisticated systems. These solutions are not only agile and scalable, but also provide a solid foundation for enterprise operations of any scale.
